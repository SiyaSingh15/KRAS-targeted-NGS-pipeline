Primer Design Parameters (Primer3)

Primer Length:
Min: 18 bp
Optimal: 20 bp
Max: 23 bp

Melting Temperature (Tm):
Min: 57°C
Optimal: 60°C
Max: 63°C

GC Content:
Min: 30%
Max: 70%

Product Size Range:
400–600 bp

Design Considerations:
- Avoid primer-dimer formation
- Maintain similar melting temperatures
- Ensure stable GC content
- Enable compatibility with paired-end sequencing
